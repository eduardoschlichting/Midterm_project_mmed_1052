About me

Who am I:

Hey! My name is Eduardo Schlichting, but you can call me Dui! :D

I'm Brazilian, 20 years old, and I'm currently living in London, Ontario, Canada!

Passionated about Design, I bring new interactive experiences to people through Interfaces and Brands.

Before I came to Canada, I lived in a small town called Bundoran, in Ireland, for almost 2 years.
There I did a language exchange program so I could learn English a bit better :D. I studied Graphic Design for 2 years in Recife, Brazil.
Since then, I've worked with a variety of clients from Brazil and even from other countries, as Ireland and the USA.
I'm focused in Brand Design but I love Motion Graphics; in the past year I started to do some experimental jobs in UI/UX Design
and Web Design, and it was there that I discovered a new passion haha.So now, I'm Studing Interactive Media Design at Fanshawe!

To be a bit more personal, I'm passionate by dogs and cats! I have 2 dogs, a "sausage dog" called Willy Wonka and
a Hottweiler called Hot! I also have a black cat called Gatinha (little cat, in english. And yeah, we're super creative with names at
my house haha). To be fair Hot and Willy are from my brother, but I lived for so long with them that I consider them being mine too haha.
I love spend my free time playing games, listening to music and travelling.

And That's me!
